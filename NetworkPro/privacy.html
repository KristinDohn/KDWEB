01 Who We Are

NetworkPro is a personal networking tracker designed to help job seekers manage professional contacts, track outreach, and stay on top of their job search pipeline. The app is operated by Kristin Dohn as a personal project.

02 What We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account credentials: Your email address and a hashed password, used solely to authenticate you.
  • Contact & networking data: Information you enter into the app — contact names, companies, notes, meeting logs, and related data. This is your data; you create it and control it.
  • Technical data: Basic server logs (IP address, browser type, request timestamps) generated automatically when you use the app. These are used only for debugging and security monitoring.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

03 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for exactly one purpose: making the app work for you.

  • Your email and password authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Your networking data is synced to the cloud so you can access it across devices.
  • Your email may be used to send you a password-reset link if you request one.
  • Server logs help us identify bugs, outages, and unauthorized access attempts.

04 What We Don't Do

To be explicit about things we will never do:

  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing platforms.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • We do not send marketing emails or newsletters.
  • We do not profile you or build behavioral models about you.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 6 below.

05 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, a managed cloud database platform with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed before storage.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

06 Third-Party Services

NetworkPro uses a small number of third-party infrastructure providers to operate:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • GitHub Pages — static file hosting for the web app frontend. GitHub Privacy Policy →
  • Resend — transactional email (password resets, access requests). Resend Privacy Policy →

These providers process data only to the extent necessary to deliver their services. We do not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers.

07 Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All the data you've entered is visible directly within the app.
  • Correction: You can edit or update any information you've entered at any time.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 11).
  • Export: If you'd like a copy of your data, contact us and we'll provide it in a readable format.

08 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes, then automatically deleted.

09 Children's Privacy

NetworkPro is intended for adults engaged in professional networking and job searching. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10 Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also send a notice to the email address associated with your account. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
